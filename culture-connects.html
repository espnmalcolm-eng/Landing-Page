01 — What this is

No stiff handshakes. No name tag lanyards. No boring panels. Just the culture doing what it does.

100 of Austin's most expressive, ambitious, and culture-forward professionals in one room — dressed how they want, connecting how they want, building relationships that actually last.

If you sit at the intersection of fashion, business, and Black & Brown excellence — this room was built for you.

Vol. 2 of a series coming to Houston, Dallas, and beyond. Get in while it's intimate.

In two years, you'll be telling people you were there from the jump.

02 — What's happening

Three ways to show up.

01

Networking Bingo

Nine squares. Nine real challenges designed to get you off your phone and into the room. Find someone who designs their own clothes. Give a genuine compliment on a fit. Connect with a content creator. Exchange info with a photographer. Complete your card and you might walk away with a swag bag. Two rounds. Two winners. All community.

02

Fit Checks & Sneaker Cam

Your outfit is your opener. Host Kee works the room from jump — live fit checks, capturing standout looks, putting the best fits and freshest kicks on camera in real time. Midway through, standout guests get pulled for quick-fire interviews: your style origin, your brand, your creative journey. The footage goes straight to social.

03

Vendor Tables

Three curated Black-owned brands. Their racks and tables live inside KickingIt ATX. Shop, discover, and support while you network.

03 — The panel

The mixer is the warmup.
The panel is where the real education begins.

Immediately following close out, Culture Connects: ATX transitions into an exclusive fashion industry panel — a raw, unfiltered conversation with real professionals who have built careers, brands, and businesses at the intersection of fashion, culture, and entrepreneurship.

  • How to break into fashion as an independent creative
  • Building and monetizing your personal brand
  • The business side: from production to distribution
  • Navigating the industry as a Black & Brown entrepreneur
  • Turning your passion for style into a sustainable career

Come with questions. Leave with answers, connections, and a clear next step for your creative journey.
